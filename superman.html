SUPERMAN:


Superman (real name Clark Kent, born Kal-El) is one of the last children
of Krypton, sent as the dying planet's last hope to Earth, where he grew
to become its kind, noble protector. Using his flight, enhanced strength,
heat vision, and various other powers, he protects the planet and the
universe from powerful threats, all while inspiring hope into others. He
is also a founding member of the Justice League, and the father of Jon
Kent, the second Superman.
