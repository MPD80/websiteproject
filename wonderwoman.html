WONDER
WOMAN:


Princess Diana is Wonder Woman, the daughter of Hippolyta,
Queen of the Amazons, and Zeus, the mightiest of the Gods of
Olympus. Diana volunteered to leave behind her home of
Themyscira and champion the Amazons' message of peace,
fighting for justice and equality in Man's World and becoming
a founding member of the Justice League.
